§ 01 / Strategy

A systematic approach to public markets.

Arvendi applies rules-based strategies across liquid equity and futures markets. Every decision is driven by a predefined framework — entries, sizing, risk limits, and exits — not by discretion or narrative. The strategy is built to be repeatable, testable, and accountable.

01Rules-Based Execution

Positions are opened and closed according to a documented framework rather than discretionary judgment. Every trade can be explained, reviewed, and reproduced against the model that generated it.

02Liquid Markets Only

Arvendi trades exclusively in deeply liquid public markets. No private credit, no illiquid structures, no positions that cannot be exited within a single trading session under normal conditions.

03Defined Risk Framework

Position-level and portfolio-level risk limits are set in advance and monitored continuously. Drawdown thresholds trigger automatic review. Capital preservation is the first objective; return is the second.

04Transparent Reporting

Limited partners receive monthly performance reports and quarterly statements prepared by an independent fund administrator. Annual audited financials conducted by a qualified auditor upon fund launch.

§ 02 / Principles

How the firm behaves.

Strategy is what we do. Principles are how we do it. Below is how Arvendi approaches the work, the clients, and the capital — before, during, and after any trade is placed.

I

Process over prediction

We do not forecast. We identify repeatable conditions the model has seen before and act on them within predefined bounds. The edge, if there is one, lives in the discipline of the process — not in any individual call.

II

Loss is the first variable

Every position is sized to a maximum loss before it is entered. Risk limits are non-negotiable. A strategy that cannot lose well cannot win consistently.

III

Costs are the enemy

Gross returns do not compound — net returns do. We minimize frictional drag at every layer: execution quality, financing costs, operational overhead, and our own fees where possible.

IV

Transparency is the default

Limited partners receive the same information we do, at the same cadence. No selective disclosure, no performance marketing ahead of audited numbers, no surprises at quarter end.
